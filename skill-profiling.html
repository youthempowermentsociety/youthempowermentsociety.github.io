What is it?

You know what you like and what you’re good at. But do you ever find it tricky to tell others about your skills when you’re put under pressure? It happens to lots of people, especially when it comes to filling out application forms for college and attending interviews. This is why it’s a good idea to figure out your skill set. Success in life comes from having the foundation on which you can build new skill sets as needed. Skill analysis helps you understand your praiseworthy aspects and shortcomings.

There are lot of merits to non-academic skills that are usually disregarded. It gives you the complete capability of a person. We at YES honour your passion and also realize the importance of skills that is the distinguishing mark of an individual in this era of competition, multiculturalism and globalization. We give you a complete report after rigorous scrutiny of your skills. This will be useful for you to validate your resume for an interview and college applications.



Program Flow


Skill Set Analysis:
The acquired skills of the individual are enlisted and documented. Any specific skill set stipulated by the individual for their career goals or by any organization is also registered and evaluated by our team.

Skill-based Profiling:
Numerous tools are used to better diagnose individuals’ strengths and weaknesses within the context of development. It exposes the true competence and skill set of the individual.

Validation:
Certificates and assessment reports that authenticate the skill set enlisted by the individual are verified and confirmed. The document that substantiates the individual participation/recognition in various competitions, conferences, sports, activities, and services are to be submitted prior to the validation.

Internship with Experts:
Upon the validation of the skills, the individual will be arranged for an internship with the expert in the proclaimed skill which will help us understand the level of competency in the particular skill. Each skill enlisted will be separately validated with different experts in that field. The time taken for the internship varies with the number of enlisted skills and availability of the individual.

Report Summary:
After completion of all the internships, each expert’s assessment of the individual on the degree of skill is ratified and compiled by our team. A complete analysis of the individual's skill set and the validation is summarized in the report.
