Model United Nation

Before understanding about MUN, you should know what United Nation is and its function. United Nation- It’s an International organization consisting of 193 member states/countries. It works based on various committees like Security Council, General Assembly- DISEC and ECOSCO. Delegates from each country are member in these committees and they represent their country where they work together with other delegates of various countries to obtain sustainable development goals, international peace and Security.

MUN is an academic simulation of the United Nation designed to help students understand diplomacy, international relations and working of the UN itself, while acquiring skills like public speaking , debating, writing, critical thinking, team work, leadership.

The students will be assigned a particular country and committee and they have to role play that particular delegate in the particular committee. It is an academic simulation of the UN designed to help students understanding diplomacy, international relations and working of the UN itself. There are particular agendas that will be discussed in the council. End result is to find a solution which is called a resolution along with delegates in the committee.

Why should you do it?

MUN is not any normal Public Speaking skill. You will have to role play as an ambassador, envoy and delegates, which means you are learning the highest level of public speaking efficiencies. And also the highest level of rules and regulation while talking to other delegates which teaches you to on how to behave diplomatically with people. This world is all about communication, the more you can communicate, the more you become an expert in that field and eventually leading to self improvement. So to be value for yourself or your boss or for your company or anything you do, this is the must have skill you should develop and this is best provided by MUN.

It will help you shine in your professional life. An academic certificate can get you any job, but to thrive in that job you have to stand out from the rest of them. They have to show something unique. What is it going to be? That’s when having some experience of participating in conferences like MUN helps.

MUN is the biggest opportunity to build a network and to expand your networking skill. Importantly you also have multicultural exposure. In an International MUN conference, there are delegates from different countries from various religious cultural backgrounds. Students get to meet and interact which allows them to understand and empathize.

If students are lucky enough, they get to meet the real heroes, the Actual Ambassadors and representatives of UN. Meeting with them and hearing their stories on what is happening in UN and also around the world. This helps them understand the importance of diplomacy in maintain world peace and security.

It increases the awareness a student has of the surroundings. They learn to appreciate different perspectives. Discussing a particular agenda makes them an expert in the topic because it’s discussed over four days and also hearing different countries perspectives.

There are Segments in MUN called “Socials”, where students are given to express themselves in any form of art. It is also an event to socialize with other delegate to build the foundation of friendship.

By now you can understand MUN is not only about public speaking skills it’s more than that.

Memories you have in MUN becomes Memory of lifetime...

Services

1.Training :

  • Level-4 : Emissary (University Students)
    For MUNNER’s who are participating at university levels. Both beginners and experiences delegates will be guided during the training sessions. Additional to the training sessions they will be given experienced practice for their socials at MUN conference.
  • Level-3 : Secretary General (Minimum of 5 MUN experience)
    For MUNNER’s who have participated over five MUN’s and has a prodigious knowledge on MUN. They will be guided to understand critical key points to impact the MUN session. This will help them prepare their offense and defence strategies.
  • Level-2 : Permanent Representatives (Minimum of 2 MUN experience)
    For MUNNER’s who have participated in two or more MUN’s. They will be assisted to improve their analysis skills and to prepare a exhaustive research binder for the delegate to be vigilant and invincible during the MUN conference.
  • Level-1 : Envoy (2 MUN experience)
    For MUNNER’s who have participated in two MUN’s. Since they have an idea of what MUN is all about, we start with emphasizing the importance of international diplomacy and its governance. This helps them have a comprehensive study of world politics.
  • Level-0 : Delegate ( 0 MUN experience)
    For beginners who have no experience in MUN. We start with general introduction to MUN and its rules of procedures. We take immense care in laying a strong foundation for them to sustain and progress in MUN.

Each Level has different features integrated to the program based on the student’s capability and mentors feedback.

To know more about the flow of program

Program Flow

Introduction to United Nation and its functions: Overview of the UN and its role in fixing the issues confronting humanity in the 21st Century. And also, an overview of its subsidiary organs and their function.

Introduction to International Politics - Treaties/Conventions/Foreign Policies: Synopsis on how the world politics operates and on various important happenings that impacted the world history.

Importance of International Diplomacy and Governance: Emphasises the diverse role a diplomat plays in order to maintain peace and security among nations. Their struggle and pain to seek world order.

Introduction to Model United Nations and Rules of Procedure (General): It teaches the rules on how the MUN is conducted and procedures that has to be followed by a delegate.

Introduction to the Committee/Council – Functions and Mandates: An overview of the council structure and the power it exercises in policy making of UN. Important actions taken by the council and its resolutions.

Introduction to the agenda (General) - Overview of the delegate guide:A thorough review of the study guide given to the delegate which gives the overview of the expectation by the chairperson on how the MUN session should advance.

Understanding the Country – Geography/Governance/Economy/Culture: Synopsis of the country allocated to an individual delegate on various aspects like physical features, demographic composition, allies and trading.

Research on Past, Present & Future phase of the agenda – Regional and International: To provide a design of actions taken by the delegate’s country on the given agenda to help them understand their country’s stance on the agenda.

Offense and Defence Strategy: Analysis on numerous perspectives and other country’s action on the given agenda to conduct an engaging MUN session.

Exploring the solutions: An important goal of the MUN is to draft a resolution on various measures that can be taken to prevent/control the problem discussed in the agenda. Provides incentive to the students to formulate solutions.

Program Features

• Practising Public Speaking Skills • Learn Advanced skills and techniques • MUN simulations • Developing your advanced leadership • Icebreakers and games • Agenda Updates • Research Skills • Discussions • Feedback from MUN mentors

2.Workshops :

Workshop- MUNdram organizes a series of workshops for students both at school and university level. These workshops are designed to provide researchers across all aspects of MUN with specialized skills and knowledge in seeking guidance from experienced mentors and delegates. YES invests considerable resources in the development, production and improvement of MUNdram Certified Workshops. The two day workshop is taught by qualified instructors and the resources used are drafted favouring both beginner and experienced students. It is also peer-reviewed and updated periodically.

3.Summer Camps :

We exclusively offer a traditional three week program; campers arrive and depart together, enabling them to form lasting friendships. A complete summer camp experience while learning new skills and learning all about MUN. Various training features are integrated in the camp to enable the students understand MUN and to build teamwork, diplomacy and cooperation. Our camp programming is designed to combat summer learning loss, the effect of losing educational ground during the time children are out of school. Structured reading and research time is built into the regular camp day to ensure that students maintain the academic gains they made during the school year. The camp participants either maintain or improve their reading comprehension over the summer and ready to start school with renewed academic confidence.

4.MUN Collaboration :

High trust environments foster the collaboration and teamwork required for success in the new global economy. Different than the traditional approaches of coordination and cooperation, real collaboration creates the key opportunity model of today’s world. We at YES value this trust bestowed up on us by our patrons and honour it by providing an opportunity to collaborate with us for mutual benefits. Upon collaboration we share our resources and tools for the development of the students by helping the school to establish regional MUN clubs/institution at their premises. This serves the students with seamless comprehensive services from our multiple programs without repeated registration procedures, waiting periods, or other administrative barriers.

Counselling Academy

It is a certified training program exclusively designed for teachers and parents who are interested to learn about MUN to guide their students/children. No prior MUN experience is required to attend this program. Comprehensive guidance will be provided to the pupil with adequate resources and tools. The pupil should be over 25 years of age and should be either a faculty at the school or a parent whose ward is going to participate in an upcoming MUN. This training will be helpful for the pupil to establish a MUN club/institution at their school. The certification* is done only after prior exams and is issued only upon successful completion of the training by the pupil.

English League

It is known to all that English is the window to the outside world. It is a universal language and the most recognized and spoken language. Without English one cannot be internationally accepted, it is even mandatory to know English while seeking any kind of employment. So at YES we offer this program which is an enabling program that aims to give students a formal and methodical exposure to professional communication skills. Our English League training will enable the students to converse fluently in the language and communicate better with their peers. Our program is specifically tailored to the needs of the students in improving their public speaking skills.
