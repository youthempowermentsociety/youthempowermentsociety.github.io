EMPOWER-THRIVE-LEAD-PERSUADE-MOTIVATE

We at Youth Empowerment Society (YES) inspire and encourage our young minds to promote intergenerational equity, civic engagement and democracy building. We at YES try to Empower, thrive, lead, persuade and motivate our youth generation for progression and development of the world. At YES we serve the students with resources to empower themselves and the society. We provide the perfect platform for the students to learn and apply various skills. We have incorporated methods to improve their intellectual, cognitive, athletic and academic ability of a student both at the school and university level.

Creativity, activity, service (CAS) is at the heart of the YES projects. With its holistic approach, CAS is designed to strengthen and extend students’ personal and interpersonal learning from the various training programs that we offer.CAS is organized around the three strands of Creativity-exploring and extending ideas leading to an original or interpretive product or performance, Activity-physical exertion contributing to a healthy lifestyle, Service-collaborative and reciprocal engagement with the community in response to an authentic need.

Our Projects

CAREER DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

Deciding the career is one of the most important decisions of a person’s life. A person spends approximately 70 % of their life working. Being successful at your career not only involves earning a 10 digit amount but it incorporates appreciation, achievements, good performance. So we help you to set a career strategy and plan your actions accordingly. We follow a methodical approach in developing your career path by meticulous steps.

MUNDRAM

MUN is an academic simulation of the United Nation designed to help students understand diplomacy, international relations and working of the UN itself, while acquiring skills like public speaking , debating, writing, critical thinking, team work, leadership. We offer the ideal resources and tools for the students to learn and explore MUN. We provide extensive mentorship in our training sessions.
