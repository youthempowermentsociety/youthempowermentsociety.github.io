Who we Are?

Youth empowerment is an attitudinal, structural, and cultural process whereby young people gain the ability, authority, and agency to make decisions and implement change in their own lives and the lives of other people. The future belongs to the young generation and it is imperative that today’s youth empower themselves with knowledge and information which will give them the strength and conviction to stand up and demand the right course in achieving sustainable development. We at Youth Empowerment Society (YES) inspire and encourage our young minds to promote intergenerational equity, civic engagement and democracy building.

We at YES try to Empower, thrive, lead, persuade and motivate our youth generation for progression and development of the world. At YES we serve the students with resources to empower themselves and the society. We provide the perfect platform for the students to learn and apply various skills. We have incorporated methods to improve their intellectual, cognitive, athletic and academic ability of a student both at the school and university level. We strongly believe in our motto “Be your own change” and in changing the world by one student at a time.

A Country’s true development is in the development of the regional and rural communities. The students from these regions are likely to be typecast as the non-elite population of the country. They are deprived of the opportunity to prove their own skills. Our aim at YES is to bring these students into the limelight.

“The world now has the largest generation of young people in history. I place great hope in their power to shape our future,”

- Ban Ki-moon (Former United Nations Secretary-General)



What do we Do?

MUNdram -MUN is an academic simulation of the United Nation designed to help students understand diplomacy, international relations and working of the UN itself, while acquiring skills like public speaking , debating, writing, critical thinking, team work, leadership. We offer the ideal resources and tools for the students to learn and explore MUN. We provide extensive mentorship in our training sessions.

Career Development Program- Deciding the career is one of the most important decisions of a person’s life. A person spends approximately 70 % of their life working. Being successful at your career not only involves earning a 10 digit amount but it incorporates appreciation, achievements, good performance. So we help you to set a career strategy and plan your actions accordingly. We follow a methodical approach in developing your career path by meticulous steps.

Skill Analysis – There are lot of merits to non-academic skills that are usually disregarded. It gives you the complete capability of a person. We at YES, honour your passion and also realize the importance of skills that is the distinguishing mark of an individual in this era of competition, multiculturalism and globalization. We give you a complete report after rigorous scrutiny of your skills. This will be useful for you to validate your resume for an interview and college applications.
