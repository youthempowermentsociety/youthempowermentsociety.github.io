Who we Are?

“The world now has the largest generation of young people in history. I place great hope in their power to shape our future,” Former United Nations Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon

Youth empowerment is an attitudinal, structural, and cultural process whereby young people gain the ability, authority, and agency to make decisions and implement change in their own lives and the lives of other people. The future belongs to the young generation and it is imperative that today’s youth empower themselves with knowledge and information which will give them the strength and conviction to stand up and demand the right course in achieving sustainable development. We at Youth Empowerment Society (YES) inspire and encourage our young minds to promote intergenerational equity, civic engagement and democracy building.

We at YES try to Empower, thrive, lead, persuade and motivate our youth generation for progression and development of the world. At YES we serve the students with resources to empower themselves and the society. We provide the perfect platform for the students to learn and apply various skills. We have incorporated methods to improve their intellectual, cognitive, athletic and academic ability of a student both at the school and university level. We strongly believe in our motto “Be your own change” and in changing the world by one student at a time.

A Country’s true development is in the development of the regional and rural communities. The students from these regions are likely to be typecast as the non-elite population of the country. They are deprived of the opportunity to prove their own skills. Our aim at YES is to bring these students into the limelight.
